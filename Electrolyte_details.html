Electrolyte - Analyzers

PA-100 Electrolyte Analyzer

Enquire Now

Overview

Promeas PA-100 Electrolyte Analyzer is a semi - automated, microprocessor controlled electrolyte system that uses ISE technology to make electrolyte measurements. The PA-100 product measures various combinations of Sodium, Potassium, Ionized Calcium, Ionized Calcium, Lithium, pH and Chloride in whole blood, serum, plasma and urine. Promea's PA-100 Electrolyte Analyzer is indigenously designed to meet the needs of small to medium-size laboratories using the innovative ISE technology. PA-100 Electrolyte Analyzers improve lab productivity while delivering sample results efficiently & economically.
Maintenance free electrode design ensures economical operation and low cost per sample in our Analyzers. Maintenance of Promeas Electrolyte Analyzers is limited to the replacement of consumables.
