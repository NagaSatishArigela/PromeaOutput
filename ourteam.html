Our Team

Dr. Rajesh Tummuru

Dr. Rajesh has obtained an MBA at Vanderbilt University - Owen Graduate School of Management with a focus in healthcare & strategy and an M.B.B.S degree from MediCiti Institute of Medical Sciences.

His passion, dedication as well as his exposure to the global health care landscape have laid foundation to many health care & diagnostic startup/s which have the potential to make an immence contribution to the global health care & diagnostic needs to attain quality diagnostics at affordable cost.

Mr. Pavan Chandra Nagoor

Mr. Pavan has Mastered in Business Administration with Honours in Hardware Engineering and Networking with rich experience in the field of Strategic Planning, Business Administration as well as Human Resource Management System.

He was a Commercial Leader with demonstrated history of growing business in the In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) & Health Care Industry with innovative "Go To Market" Strategies. His experience as well as exposure in versatile Job Profiles along with positive attitude and a passion has given him an opportunity to establish and accordingly relish himself as a trailblazer in all the organizations which he has been associated with.

Mr. Piyush Bhavsar

Mr. Piyush has mastered in Chemistry and carries rich experience in manufacturing of versatile infusion products and injectables which includes Non PVC bags, Multi chamber bags , Specialty products, Amino Acid products, FAT products.

He brings 30 years of experience in plant management and operations. His rich experience blended with exposure to all the areas of plant operations have made him a reputable leader with the capability to handle diverse operations involved in the injectable manufacturing plants.
