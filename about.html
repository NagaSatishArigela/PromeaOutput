Promea Therapeutics is an innovative medical device manufacturing company specializing in Life Science products, Diagnostics; including Clinical Chemistry, Immunodiagnostics, Molecular Diagnostics, and Infusion Therapies. We aim to make a significant difference to global healthcare markets through greater access to quality diagnostics as well as critical care parenterals.

We have a highly skilled & dedicated R&D team who are in constant pursuit of precision, quality, and innovation. Our state-ofthe-art manufacturing facilities drive quality production of our innovative Invitro Diagnostic Medical Devices, Large & Small Volume Parentals and Biologics to satisfy the needs of our customers.

  • OUR VISION

    Promea Therapeutics intends to become a global healthcare company and to be recognized as reliable global integrated diagnostic, healthcare and pharmaceutical company through Innovation, Quality and Competence.

    We aspire to be the customers preferred and most trusted medical, healthcare & Diagnostic device manufacturer uplifting the quality and dwindling the cost of health care for patients.

  • OUR MISSION

    Our mission is to develop high quality medical, healthcare & Diagnostic products and to make them available in the remotest parts of the planet at an affordable price which can easily be accessible to all sections of the medical community to benefit human health.

    We intend to uphold our social responsibilities by delivering highest standard healthcare services to all segments of society without compromising on our core values of commitment. Promea Therapeutics aims to become a globally acclaimed pharmaceutical company through development and introduction of wide Portfolio of Diagnostics, Biologics, Pharmaceutical Products.

  • OUR CORE VALUES

    Customer Priority Approach : We believe that the customer is the driving force behind all we do and we strive to anticipate and meet their needs

    Continuous Improvement : We are dedicated to the pursuit of perfection, which prerequisites our customers, our vendors, and our employees.

    Commitment : We do what we say. We believe in team work to attain the long-term interests of our company.

    Courage : We are courageous and prepared to take risks to find new paths.

    Care : We care about people, health, safety, society and the environment.
